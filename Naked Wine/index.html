This is us

Wine without a Dresscode

We are a great team of five euphoric wine lovers from all over the world who work with passion to give you the best wine experience possible. Wine is simply great - thats why we think a suit or an evening dress is not mandatory to enjoy wine. Wine is just as great wearing your boyfriends hoody. Matter of fact; some of us even drink wine in their pyjamas - Wine without a Dresscode

Our store is located in a small side alley in heart of Sydney. We have local wines, but also offer an attractive selection from overseas. We offer random wine tastings on the regular, so make sure to follow us on social media to get the latest infos. Also if you are in the neighborhood, make sure to drop by for glass on the go, a small-talk or for a nice bottle for the weekend! See you soon!

We are super cool, you should say Hi!
